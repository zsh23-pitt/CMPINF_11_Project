Datasets Used:

neighborhoods.csv - City of Pittsburgh Neighborhoods, base information on
    neighborhoods, used for reference
    
facilities.csv - City of Pittsburgh Facilities, data from the department 
    of Public Works about various buildings maintained by the city

trees.csv - City of Pittsburgh Trees, data from Department of Public Works
    Forestry division about trees maintained by the city

incidents_2024_thru_mar2026.xls- City of Pittsburgh Monthly Criminal Activity,       data from Bureau of Police crime incidence reports

Overview:
This is a group project for the class Big Ideas in Computing and Information,
where we create a metric based on a few data sets of our choice to determine
the best neighborhood in Pittsburgh.
